Game Overview
Ultimate Tic Tac Toe is a strategic board game played on a 3x3 grid of 3x3 grids. Two players, X and O, take turns marking a square on the board. The first player to get three in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) in a single grid wins that grid. The first player to win three grids in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins the game.

Game Rules
1. GamePlay: Players take turns marking a square on the board. The player who plays as X makes the first move.
2. Grid Winning: A player wins a grid by getting three of their marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) within that grid.
3. Game Winning: A player wins the game by winning three grids in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).
4. Next Move: After a player wins a grid, their next move will determine which grid their opponent can play in. The opponent can play in the grid corresponding to the square marked by the first player.
5. Free Play: If a player is sent to a grid that has already been won or is full, they can play in any available grid.

Strategies
1. Control the Center: The center grid is the most important grid in the game. Try to win the center grid or block your opponent from winning it.
2. Block Your Opponent: Pay attention to your opponent's moves and try to block their winning lines.
3. Build Multiple Winning Lines: Try to build multiple winning lines in different grids to increase your chances of winning.

Tips for Beginners
1. Start with the Center Grid: Try to make your first move in the center grid to gain an advantage.
2. Focus on Winning Grids: Prioritize winning individual grids over blocking your opponent's winning lines.
3. Plan Ahead: Think about your opponent's potential moves and plan your strategy accordingly.

By following these rules and strategies, you can improve your chances of winning at Ultimate Tic Tac Toe.
